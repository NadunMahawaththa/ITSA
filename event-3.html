Intro'26

November 14, 2025 Faculty 8th Floor

INTRO is the official welcome programme organised by the Information Technology Student Association (ITSA) of the University of Kelaniya. It marks the very first step of an exciting university journey, offering incoming students a space to connect, explore, and feel at home within the vibrant ITSA community while smoothly transitioning into campus life.

More than just an event, INTRO reflects the true spirit, unity, and energy of ITSA. Every activity is thoughtfully designed to make new members feel genuinely welcomed, appreciated, and confident from the moment they arrive. As a recognised student association, ITSA focuses on building meaningful relationships, encouraging teamwork, and creating a strong sense of belonging that supports students both socially and academically throughout their university experience.

The programme blends fun with purpose. Through interactive games, team challenges, ice-breaking sessions, and engaging activities, participants will not only laugh and enjoy themselves, but also develop communication, leadership, and collaboration skills. It is a chance to step beyond comfort zones, discover hidden talents, form lasting friendships, and experience the lively culture that defines the ITSA family.

Beyond the excitement, INTRO aims to inspire students to become active and confident contributors to ITSA. By bringing everyone together in a positive and energetic environment, the event builds a foundation for creativity, responsibility, and personal growth. It encourages students to take part in future projects, leadership roles, and initiatives, shaping individuals who will grow with ITSA and contribute meaningfully to the university community and beyond.
