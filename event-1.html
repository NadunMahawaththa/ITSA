නැණසවිය

“නැණසවිය” is a community outreach initiative organised by the Information Technology Student Association (ITSA) of the University of Kelaniya. It reflects the association’s commitment to using student knowledge and skills to support school students and create meaningful social impact beyond the university environment.

නැණසවිය represents a step beyond classroom learning by showing how university students can share their knowledge with the wider community. Through this initiative, ITSA highlights that education should be accessible to everyone, including students who may not have equal academic resources. As a recognised student association, ITSA brings structure, responsibility, and careful planning to the programme, ensuring that it is a well-organised effort focused on delivering real educational value rather than a one-time visit.

The project aims to conduct a seminar series for O/L students in rural and underprivileged schools, recognising the importance of this stage in a student’s academic journey. O/L examinations play a major role in shaping future educational pathways, and timely guidance can strongly influence both academic performance and career decisions. By focusing on rural and underprivileged schools, the programme helps reduce the gap in educational opportunities and provides consistent academic support through structured sessions.

නැණසවිය also seeks to improve students’ academic performance, confidence, and awareness of future educational opportunities. Beyond subject knowledge, the sessions will focus on study techniques, time management, and exam preparation strategies while also inspiring students to think about higher education and career possibilities. Through knowledge sharing and mentorship, ITSA hopes to empower young learners, motivate them to achieve their goals, and contribute positively to long-term social development.
