Meet our experienced advisors guiding our vision and growth.

Prof. K. G. H. Dhammika Weerasinghe

B.Sc. (Special) (Kel'ya), MSc (Oakland, USA), PhD (Oakland, USA)

Dean of the Faculty

"The Information Technology Student Association (ITSA) proudly represents students of the Department of Software Engineering, established in December 2015 with the Faculty of Computing and Technology. Our department is committed to excellence in software engineering through innovation, knowledge sharing, and capacity building for the socio-economic development of the nation. As students of the BCS and BICT degree programmes, including specializations such as Software Systems, Games and Animation, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence, we are part of a forward-thinking academic community. Our learning journey emphasizes teamwork, creativity, collaboration, and hands-on experience in designing real-world software solutions. ITSA serves as a platform to support your academic growth, leadership development, and technical excellence. We encourage all members to actively participate, innovate boldly, and work together to become future-ready professionals who can create high-quality, impactful, and cost-effective technology solutions. Together, let us learn, lead, and shape the future of technology."
