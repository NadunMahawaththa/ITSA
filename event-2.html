ITSA Back to Events

Innovista'26

The ITSA is excited to organize an industry engagement event for students in the Software Engineering, Networking, and Game & Animation pathways. This program brings an invited IT company to campus to conduct specialized sessions for each pathway, helping students gain real-world exposure and professional guidance. The event focuses on preparing students for the modern IT industry by connecting academic learning with practical experience.

This event aims to give students a clear understanding of how their chosen pathway connects to the real IT industry. Industry professionals will conduct sessions for each pathway and share insights on technologies, tools, and skills required in the workplace.

In addition to technical knowledge, the event will emphasize career readiness and professional development. Company representatives will share insights on internship opportunities, portfolio building, teamwork, communication skills, and workplace culture. Students will have the opportunity to interact directly with professionals, ask questions, and receive guidance on career planning within their chosen pathways. This interaction helps students build confidence, understand industry trends, and prepare themselves for future employment in the IT sector.

The event also encourages collaboration and knowledge sharing among students from all three pathways. By bringing Software, Networking, and Game & Animation students together on one platform, the ITSA aims to create a supportive environment where students can learn from each other’s interests and skills. Such exposure can inspire interdisciplinary projects, innovation, and creative problem-solving. The session will motivate students to stay updated with industry advancements, improve their technical expertise, and actively participate in future IT-related activities and workshops.
